PROGRAMS WE OFFER

LET'S SHAPE THE FUTURE OF SPORTS TOGETHER

We believe the traditional model of youth sports is not built to bring out the best in young athletes, or to deliver the most to its fans. So we developed an all-new model for youth athletes and sports - and for you.

Our camp, created on May 24, 2021, focuses mainly on soccer and basketball. It's designed for young men and women athletes between the ages of 15 and 18. We are using this innovative athlete-centric model to create a unique training environment across these sports.

As a community-driven initiative, we are mission-driven and founded on the values of innovation, inclusion, and excellence. We aim to empower athletes as leaders, promote inclusive participation focused on long-term athletic and personal well-being of athletes, and bring fans closer to the game by innovating on and off the field.

We're committed to responsible coaching, stewardship of our athletes, and a program rooted in innovation, efficiency, and durability. We make athletes designed to last generations. Years after our founding, we're still exploring new territory every day: creating incredible Skills, inspired Solutions, and never-ending Innovations for your next game under the open sky.

We are incredibly proud of what our community of athletes, coaches, and partners have already accomplished in just a few short years. And the best part is, we're just getting started. Join us as we embark on this exciting journey, shaping the future of sports, one athlete at a time. Welcome to our family!

SOCCER

MEN AND WOMEN TEAM

We are elevating the narratives of diverse athletes in our camp and creating more opportunities for young athletes of all backgrounds to envision themselves as professional athletes. Our camp, open to everyone aged 15 - 18 for trials, ensures selected individuals go through rigorous training with our dedicated coaches. This is for both young men and women.

BASKETBALL

MEN AND WOMEN TEAM

We are amplifying the stories of diverse athletes in our basketball camp and creating more opportunities for young athletes of all backgrounds to envision themselves as professional basketball players. Our basketball camp, open to everyone aged 15 - 18 for trials, ensures selected individuals go through rigorous training with our dedicated basketball coaches. This is for both young men and women.

CONNECTION

TEAM BUILDING

We are fostering camaraderie both on the soccer field and the basketball court. We encourage our athletes, both young men and women, to champion the causes they hold dear, providing them with guidance and resources. We cultivate their leadership skills, serving as a catalyst for community development and civic advancement. We celebrate our athletes and bring their stories to life through art, with original portraits created by emerging artists. This not only adds a unique touch to our camp but also allows our athletes to see themselves as works of art, inspiring them to strive for greatness. This gives them a chance to learn from the best, gain valuable insights, and see firsthand what it takes to excel in soccer and basketball.
