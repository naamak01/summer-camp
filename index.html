ABOUT US

WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

Our passion is the beautiful game of soccer and the dynamic sport of basketball. We adapt the lessons learned on the pitch and the court for the rigors of life's challenges, whether you're aiming for a goal or shooting for a basket. We believe sports bring us together, shape our character, and, mysteriously, always feel more exhilarating when played under the open sky.

Founder Nana Amakye, an American who spent his childhood in Lincoln, Nebraska, is no stranger to adventure: Amakye spent his youth playing in the European League before ending his career in the Major League Soccer (MLS). Now, he has turned his focus to giving back to the community that shaped him, starting this camp to help hone and enhance teens' skills in soccer and basketball.

From his humble beginnings to his tough-as-nails attitude on the field, Amakye has always had a deep love for the game. He played a little basketball and loved it, and now he wants to share that love with the next generation.

We're committed to responsible coaching, stewardship of our athletes, and a program rooted in innovation, efficiency, and durability. We make athletes designed to last generations. Years after our founding, we're still exploring new territory every day: creating incredible Skills, inspired Solutions, and never-ending Innovations for your next game under the open sky.

Join us as we embark on this exciting journey, shaping the future of sports, one athlete at a time. Welcome to our family!

FAQs

What information is needed for registration?

Name, Age, Guardian Phone Number, Guardian Name, Guardian Relations, and any Allergies or Special Accommodations.

What is the deadline for registration?

The deadline for registration is currently set at May 29th. If anything changes, you will be updated.

How long will this summer camp be?

Each camp has has the same duration which can be found on the Calendar Page. Specific information can also be found on the Calendar Page including address.

What is the age Requirement for this camp?

The ages required will be between the ages of 15 and 18. This is to target mainly high school students.

Are meals provided during the camp?

No. This will be a day camp where students or parents will bring their children for the dates of the scheduled activities.
